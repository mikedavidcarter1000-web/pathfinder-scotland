You need 3–5 Highers depending on the university, always including English (or an English-based subject) and usually a science (Biology preferred). National 5 Maths and English are required everywhere.

## Nursing entry requirements across Scotland

### University of Glasgow — BN

Standard **AABBB** (S6), WA **BBBB** guaranteed with Top-Up / Summer School. Two Higher sciences required, interview.

### University of Edinburgh — BN

Standard **ABBB**, WA **BBBB** (Plus Flag). Interview required.

### University of Dundee — BN

Standard **AABB**, WA **BBBC–BBBB**. Science subject, interview.

### University of Stirling — BN Adult / Mental Health

Standard **AABB**, WA **BBBB**. Science subject, interview. SWAP accepted.

### Robert Gordon University — BN Dual Registration

Standard **BBBC**, WA **BBC–BCC**. Interview + PVG.

### Edinburgh Napier — BN Adult / Child

Standard **BBC** (3 Highers), WA **BCC**. English-based + science, interview.

### Glasgow Caledonian — BN

Standard **BCC** (3 Highers incl. English), WA **CCC**. PVG + health screen. Lowest standard entry in Scotland.

### University of the West of Scotland — BN Adult / Mental Health

Standard **ABBB** (incl. Biology), WA **BBBB**. Interview + PVG. Largest nursing provider.

### Queen Margaret University — BN Adult

Standard **BBBB**, WA **BBBC**. English-based + science, interview.

### Abertay University — BN Mental Health

Standard **BBBB**, WA **BBC** (3 Highers). PVG + interview.

### UHI — BN

Standard **BBBC**, WA contextual reductions. Interview.

## What counts as an "English-based subject"?

Most universities accept the following in place of Higher English:

- English
- History
- Modern Studies
- Philosophy
- RMPS
- Psychology
- Sociology
- Politics

Always check the specific course page — a few programmes (Glasgow especially) insist on English itself.

## What counts as a science?

Most universities want at least one science Higher. Biology is most commonly listed but Chemistry, Physics, and Human Biology are accepted at many places. GCU and Abertay have no specific science Higher requirement.

## The college route

- **HNC Care and Administrative Practice** or **HNC Social Services**, then articulate into Year 2.
- **SWAP Access to Nursing** for mature students.

Strong articulation routes at UWS, GCU, RGU, and Napier.

## Non-negotiable requirements

Every nursing programme in Scotland requires:

- PVG scheme membership
- Occupational health screening
- An interview
- National 5 Maths and English at C or above

## Specialisms from day one

You apply to a specific field, not "Nursing" in general:

- **Mental Health** — Stirling, UWS, Abertay, QMU
- **Child** — Edinburgh Napier
- **Dual Registration (Adult + Mental Health)** — RGU only

All degrees are 4 years with NHS Scotland placements. Free tuition through SAAS.

## What to do next

1. **Pick 3–5 universities.** Look at the field of nursing each one offers.
2. **Check your N5 Maths and English.** A C is the floor — no exceptions.
3. **Get your PVG and interview prep sorted early** — the deadlines come up fast.

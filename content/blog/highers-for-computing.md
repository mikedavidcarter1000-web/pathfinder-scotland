**Higher Maths is essential.** Every computing degree requires or strongly recommends it. Higher Computing Science is helpful but not always required.

## Computing entry across Scottish universities

### University of Edinburgh — BSc Computer Science / AI

Standard **AAAA–AAAB**, WA **AAAB–AABB**. Maths A.

### University of Glasgow — BSc

Standard **AAABB**, WA **BBBB** guaranteed. Maths at B.

### University of St Andrews — BSc

Standard **AAAB–AAAAB**, WA **AABB–AABBB** or **BBBB** Gateway.

### University of Aberdeen — BSc

Standard **BBBB**, WA **BBC** guaranteed unconditional.

### University of Strathclyde — BSc

Standard **AABB–AAAB**, WA **ABBB–AABB**.

### University of Dundee — BSc

Standard **AABB**, WA **BBBC–BBBB**.

### Heriot-Watt — BSc CS / AI / Data Science

Standard **AABB**, WA **BBBC** guaranteed.

### University of Stirling — BSc Computing

Standard **ABBB**, WA **BBBC**.

### Edinburgh Napier — BSc Software Engineering / Cyber

Standard **ABBB**, WA **BBBC**.

### Glasgow Caledonian — BSc Computing / AI

Standard **BBCC**, WA **BCCC**.

### Robert Gordon University — BSc

Standard **BBBB**, WA **BCCC**.

### Abertay — BSc Game Apps / Ethical Hacking

Standard **BBBB**, WA **ABB–BBC** (3 Highers).

### UWS — BSc Computing / Cyber

Standard **BCCC**, WA **CCC**.

## Do you need Higher Computing Science?

**Not at most universities.** Maths plus any technical subject works. Higher Computing Science is helpful for the topics it covers (programming, data structures, system design) but it's rarely on the formal entry requirements list.

If your school doesn't offer it, or it clashes with another subject in your column block, don't panic — you can still do a computing degree.

## Specialist routes — who offers what

- **AI** — Edinburgh, Glasgow, Heriot-Watt, GCU, Abertay
- **Cyber Security** — Napier, Abertay, GCU, UWS, RGU
- **Game Design** — Abertay, GCU, UWS
- **Data Science** — Heriot-Watt, Glasgow, Stirling, GCU

Abertay is internationally known for its games degrees and was the first university in the world to offer a Computer Games degree.

## Foundation Apprenticeships

Two FAs are directly relevant:

- **FA IT Software Development** (SCQF Level 6)
- **FA IT Hardware and System Support** (SCQF Level 6)

Both are accepted by all Scottish universities and count as a Higher.

## The college route

HNC / HND Computing then articulate into Year 2 or 3 of a degree. Typically needs 1–2 Highers to get into the HNC.

Strong articulation routes at Abertay, GCU, UWS, RGU, and Napier.

## What to do next

1. **Make sure Maths is in your S5 timetable.** Without it, the door narrows fast.
2. **Pick a specialism.** Generic CS at one university may suit you better than specialist Cyber at another, or vice versa.
3. **Look at industry placements.** Heriot-Watt, Strathclyde, and RGU all have strong sandwich-year traditions.
